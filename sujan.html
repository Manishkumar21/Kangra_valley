INTRODUCTION Tira Sujanpur is located at 31.83°N 76.50°E. It has an average elevation of 562 metres (1,844 feet). Climate here varies all through from very hot summer to cold winter. Being on the bank of river Beas, the winter is quite severe. The temperature vary from 38Deg C in summers to almost zero Deg C in winters. read more OVERVIEW Tira Sujanpur is also called Sujanpur Tira or Sujanpur Tihra located on the bank of River Beas. It is a beautiful town inhabited by Maharaja Sansar Chand Katoch who was the King of Kangra and later shifted from Kangra to Sujanpur Tira after the war with Muslim kings who wanted to capture Kangra fort. read more HISTORY Sujanpur bears its name from Sajjanpur, which means the town of honest and gentle people. Tira was later added as Maharaja Sansar Chand Katoch moved his capital from Kangra to this place and added more palaces, temples, courts and fort at the top of the hill ‘Tira’. Katoch have been kings for time immemorial and their dynasty can be traced back to 4300BC when Rajanaka Bhumi Chand founded the Katoch Dynasty. read more TEMPLES OF SUJANPUR Maharaja Sansar Chand Katoch built large palaces and temples. Some of the temples are a piece of great architecture and Kangra paintings.
